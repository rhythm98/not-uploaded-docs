Article (19) Supply of Real Estate Related Services

  1. Real Estate Related Services shall mean those that are closely linked to real estate, including:

    1. real estate experts and agent services;

    2. granting the right to possess or use real estate;

    3. services related to construction work;

  2. As an exception to the provisions of Article 15 of this Agreement, the place of supply of Real Estate Related Services shall be where the real estate is located.
