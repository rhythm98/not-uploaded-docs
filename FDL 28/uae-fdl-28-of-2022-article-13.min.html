Title 3 - Tax Procedures

Chapter 2 - Tax Agents

Article 13 - Conditions of Registration of the Tax Agent, Suspension of Registration and Deregistration

  1. The Executive Regulation shall determine the conditions, controls and procedures for registration of the Tax Agent, suspension of registration and deregistration, and the rights and obligations of the Tax Agent.

  2. The Tax Agent shall notify the Authority if he ceases to practise his profession as a Tax Agent in accordance with the controls and procedures set out in the Executive Regulation.
