Article 67 - Date of Issuance of Tax Invoices

  1. The Registrant shall issue the Tax Invoice within (14) days as of the date of the supply set forth in Article (25) and (26) of this Decree-Law.

  2. The Executive Regulations of this Decree-Law shall identify the cases that are subject to time limits other than the ones stated in Clause (1) above, or cases where the Tax Invoice shall be promptly issued, as per the controls set forth therein.
