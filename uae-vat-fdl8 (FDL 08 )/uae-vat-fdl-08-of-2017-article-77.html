Part Nine - Violations and Penalties

Article 77 - Tax Evasion

If it is proved that a person who is not a registrant acquires goods in accordance with Clause (3) of Article (48) of this Decree-Law, and claims that he is a registrant, he shall be deemed to have committed a tax evasion and shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of Federal Law No. (7) of 2017 on Tax Procedures.
