Chapter 20 - Closing provisions

Article 65 - Revenue Sharing

[See GTL Notes]

Corporate Tax and Top-up Tax revenues and Administrative Penalties collected under this Decree-Law shall be subject to sharing between the Federal Government and the Local Governments based on the provisions of a federal law in force in this regard. (See GTL Notes)

GTL Notes

Inserted vide Federal Decree Law No. 60 of 2023 dated 2 October 2023
